What is proven

The demo provides three concrete hiring signals: (1) transparent retrieval traces with similarity scores, (2) grounded drafting constrained by retrieved context, and (3) a minimal regression-style evaluation loop.

Production design choices

A production implementation adds embeddings + vector store, hybrid retrieval, reranking, strict citations policy, refusal on weak retrieval, and schema-validated outputs. CI/CD covers prompts, retrieval configs, and evaluation sets.

Evaluation harness: what recruiters look for

A credible RAG build ships with measurable signals and a repeatable loop: retrieval hit-rate, precision@k, citation correctness, faithfulness, latency, and cost per query. You maintain a suite of regression prompts and labelled failure cases, replayed on every release.

precision@k faithfulness citation correctness latency budget cost controls regression prompts

Reliability and guardrails

Real-world failure modes are addressed explicitly: prompt injection, missing context, wrong context, and over-confident answers. Reliability patterns include refusal on empty retrieval, source-based claims, structured output validation, and monitoring for drift/regressions.
