How it connects to the applied work

The “neuromorphic” signature in the applied portfolio is not “SNN-only”. It’s the discipline of making inference efficient and reliable: explicit trade-offs, robustness under constraints, and observability.

  • Efficiency-first inference: latency/cost budgets.
  • Robustness: failure modes & imperfect inputs.
  • Operability: reproducibility, monitoring hooks.
