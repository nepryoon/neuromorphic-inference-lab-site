About

Neuromorphic Inference Lab is a personal portfolio focused on production-first AI systems. “Neuromorphic” is the signature: obsession for efficient inference (latency/cost/robustness) and operability (reproducibility, monitoring, clear trade-offs).

What I build

End-to-end systems where machine learning is just one component of a reliable product: data ingestion, pipelines, model serving, observability, and user-facing interfaces.

  • NLP (RAG, assistants with citations & eval)
  • Computer Vision (classification + explainability)
  • Tabular / Forecasting (scoring + reporting + drift awareness)
  • MLOps (containerization, CI/CD, deployability)
